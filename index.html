About GenWear

GenWear is a stylish and smart band designed for students and all generations. With a built-in QR code, it connects wearers to their digital identity, educational resources, and health information instantly. It combines technology and fashion to create a seamless experience for users and institutions alike.

Key Features

Student Mode

Tracks attendance, aid, and connects to educational platforms.

Senior Mode

Includes emergency contacts, health stats, and medication reminders.

Family Sync

Share fitness goals, location check-ins, and event invites easily.

Custom QR

Each band has a unique QR code linked to a personal digital profile.
